About Pear Watch Shop

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Pear Watch Shop started with a simple mission: to bring premium quality watches to customers worldwide. What began as a small boutique in the heart of the city has grown into a trusted online destination for watch enthusiasts.

We carefully curate every watch in our collection to ensure that each timepiece meets our high standards for quality, design, and craftsmanship. Our passion for horology drives everything we do.
